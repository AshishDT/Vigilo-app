## ⭐ What Makes Vigilo Unique

- 🃏 **Collapsible/Expandable Exam Cards** – View exams at a glance or expand for full metadata.
- 🕒 **Circular Countdown Timer** – A rotating progress ring with live time digits inside.
- 📊 **Percentage Slider** – Tracks time elapsed in real time (read-only or scrubbable).
- ➕ **Quick Add Wizard** – 4-step workflow to set up new exams with optional repeat setup.
- 🧰 **Officer Tools Tab** – Log incidents, control timers, message invigilators, and export logs.
- 🌓 **Light & Dark Modes** – Automatically adapts to system appearance.
- 📂 **Local Data Storage** – Exams and logs are saved to the device.
- 📤 **Export Logs** – One-tap copy or download of time-stamped exam records.
